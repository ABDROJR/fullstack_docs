Ryan Gosling

Ryan Thomas Gosling (born November 12, 1980) is a Canadian actor.

Early life and education

Ryan Thomas Gosling was born on November 12, 1980, at St. Joseph's Hospital in London,Ontario, the son of Thomas Ray Gosling, a traveling salesman for a paper mill,and Donna, a   secretary.
Both of his parents are of part French Canadian descent, along with some German, English, Scottish, and Irish. He and his family were members of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints
,and Gosling has said that the religion influenced every aspect of their lives. Because of his father's work, they "moved around a lot" and Gosling lived in both Cornwall, Ontario and Burlington.

Acting career

In 1993, 12-year-old Gosling attended an open audition in Montreal for a revival of the Disney Channel's The Mickey Mouse Club.
He was given a two-year contract as a mouseketeer and moved to Orlando, Florida. He appeared on-screen infrequently because
other children were considered more talented.
